Dungeonborne is a dark fantasy extraction adventure where players enter dangerous dungeons, fight monsters, collect treasure, and survive against other heroes. Every decision matters, from choosing weapons and armor to judging whether an enemy can be defeated before escape becomes impossible. This Dungeonborne Damage Calculator helps you quickly estimate basic combat damage by comparing your Attack Power with a target's Defense. The formula multiplies attack by 1.2, then subtracts defense, creating a simple way to represent empowered strikes, weapon bonuses, or offensive scaling. If defense is higher than the adjusted attack, the result is shown as zero so you can clearly see when an attack may fail to break through protection. Use the calculator before a run to compare builds, test gear upgrades, evaluate risky fights, and understand how much damage your character might deal in a dungeon encounter.
