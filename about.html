📖 About Game Tools Hub

Game Tools Hub was founded to provide free, accurate, and easy-to-use damage calculators and game tools for players around the world. We understand that optimizing your character's performance is key to enjoying many RPGs and action games.

Our calculators are built using publicly available game formulas and are regularly updated to reflect game patches. We are committed to delivering reliable tools that help you make better in-game decisions.

Currently we support Shadow Arena and Dungeonborne, with more games coming soon. Our tools are completely free, and we fund the site through unobtrusive advertising.
